About Us

Your Trusted Local
Vehicle Marketplace

AutoHub is a web-based platform designed to simplify the process of purchasing, selling, and renting vehicles for users in the Pinamungajan and Toledo region.

Using our platform, locals can easily manage transactions, publish listings, and browse available vehicles from any internet-enabled device.

Join AutoHub Today

Our Mission

To provide a simple, accessible, and organized platform for vehicle transactions in Pinamungajan and Toledo — connecting buyers, sellers, and renters in one trusted marketplace.

Our Vision

To become the go-to vehicle marketplace for communities across Cebu, promoting modern, digital-first approaches to buying, selling, and renting vehicles.
